About Us

One stop solution for fashion and imitation jewelleries.

RSK Overseas is an emerging wholesale exporter company which deals in all forms of immitation jewellery, jewellery boxes, agri products, paper bags and God idols. Our team understands the requirements and needs of our customers and provides products that help you to improvise your business and eventually life.

  • We thrive in the direction to benefit our client's business by employing our expertise in the export domain.
  • We are young in the field of export business but our team comprises of peoples which are in the business since past 7 years.
  • Our products and services includes artificial jewellery, agri products, paperbags etc preparation and their handling.

Why choose us?

We understand the concerns of export business and worries of our overseas customers for providing delightful solution. We work on the mission of providing best of quality products as per customers with a vision of clients' complete satisfaction.

We are motivated by the desire to provide products that makes you feel joyful that in turn improves quality of life, that sparks enthusiasm. We believe in mutual development and growth of our business and clients.

In Short, our vision ranging from focus on the future and earning to cultural diversity which summarises our values, our strengths and our strategic orientation.
